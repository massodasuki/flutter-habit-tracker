Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

This is a simple, offline habit tracking app. We value your privacy and want you to know exactly what data is (and isn't) collected.

1. Data We Collect

None. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

2. Local Data Storage

All your habit data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. This includes:

  • Habit names you create
  • Habit colors you choose
  • Completion dates for each habit
  • Streak information

This data remains on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising services, or tracking tools.

4. Permissions

This app requires the following permissions:

  • Storage - Required for local SQLite database to store your habits

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we don't collect any data in the first place.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. Since this is an offline app with no server component, any changes would only affect how data is stored locally on your device.

8. Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete individual habits within the app
  • You can uninstall the app to remove all stored data
  • There is no cloud backup - your data exists only on your device

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please feel free to reach out.

Summary

This Habit Tracker app is designed with privacy in mind. It works completely offline, stores data only on your device, and does not collect any personal information. Use it with confidence!
